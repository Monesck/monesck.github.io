Our mission will help us gain a better understanding of this process.

.Paul: Can you tell me a little bit about the craft?

.Dr Halliday : Sure, it's the InSight Lander. Lander crafts don’t have wheels. This is extremely imp ortant to us. The first mission that landed on Mars, Viking, actually had a seismometer that stayed on the craft. So it detected the gusts of wind that made the craft shake, but it could not detect quakes precisely at all.

.Paul: I see, so the InSight also has a seismometer?

We use a robotic arm to put the seismometer in the ground and then place a wind shield on top of the seismometer to protect it from wind gusts that could be mista ken for seismic waves.
